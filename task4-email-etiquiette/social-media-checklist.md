DO'S:

1-Think before posting – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2-Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
3-Verify information – Check facts before sharing news or posts.
4-Maintain a positive digital footprint – Share content that reflects your values and goals.
5-Balance your time – Use social media in moderation to avoid distraction from studies.

DON'TS:

1-Don’t overshare personal information – Avoid posting sensitive details like address or phone number.
2-Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech – It can harm your reputation.
3-Don’t believe everything you see – Not all content online is true or reliable.
4-Don’t post impulsively – Emotional posts can lead to regret later.
5-Don’t neglect real-life responsibilities – Social media should not affect academics or health.
